Caring For Your Lashes

These are some of the tips we recommend to achieve the most glamorous, longest lasting results:

  • Keep your new lashes dry and avoid participating in activities with excessive humidity and that cause you to sweat.
  • Do not use oil-based products of any kind on or near your eyes.
  • You should avoid touching your lashes directly, and we recommend sleeping on your side or back, not face down.
